🔧 Installation Quick Guide
1
Install Minecraft Forge for your MC version from files.minecraftforge.net
2
Download the mod .jar from CurseForge or Modrinth — never use unofficial sites
3
Place .jar files in .minecraft/mods/ folder (create it if missing)
4
For Immersive Vehicles: also download separate car pack .jars and add them to /mods
5
Launch Minecraft with the Forge profile selected in the launcher
6
Craft vehicles using the Workbench recipes in-game or use Creative inventory
Frequently Asked Questions
What is the best Minecraft car mod in 2024?

Immersive Vehicles (formerly MTS) by Turbo4 is the top choice for 2024. It has an active community, hundreds of add-on car packs, realistic physics, working gauges, lights, and sounds. It supports Minecraft 1.12.2 through 1.20+. For Fabric users, Vehicular or the Create mod ecosystem are solid alternatives.

Can you get a Lamborghini or Ferrari in Minecraft?

Yes! The Supercar Pack add-on for Immersive Vehicles includes Lamborghini Aventador, Lamborghini Huracán, Ferrari 488, Ferrari LaFerrari, Bugatti Chiron, McLaren 720S, and dozens more. Each model has custom engine sounds, working speedometers, and accurate colors. Download the pack from the official MTS Discord or CurseForge.

Do Minecraft car mods work on servers?

Yes, most car mods work on multiplayer servers as long as both the server and all clients have the same mod version installed. Server admins add the mod .jar to the server's mods folder. Immersive Vehicles is especially popular on roleplay and city servers.

What Minecraft version should I use for car mods?

For the widest mod compatibility, use Minecraft 1.12.2 (Forge) if you want the biggest library of car packs, or 1.16.5 / 1.18.2 for a balance of modern features and mod support. 1.20+ versions are gaining support quickly. Avoid very new versions if a specific mod hasn't updated yet.

How do you fuel cars in Minecraft car mods?

In Immersive Vehicles, vehicles run on Biodiesel (crafted from plant matter + buckets) or Aviation Fuel depending on the vehicle type. You fill tanks using a Jerrycan or Fuel Pump. Some add-on packs require specific fuels. The Create mod integration allows automating fuel production with engineering machines.

Best Minecraft Luxury & Sports Car Mods — 2024 Complete Guide

Minecraft vehicle mods have evolved dramatically, with some modern packs delivering near-photorealistic luxury and supercar experiences. From Lamborghini Aventadors to Bugatti Chirons, BMW M-series to Ferrari 488s — the best car mods bring real-world vehicles into Minecraft with working dashboards, engine sounds, headlights, and physics.

The Immersive Vehicles (MTS) ecosystem is the gold standard. The base mod provides the vehicle physics engine, while separate add-on packs deliver specific car models. The Supercar Pack, Luxury Cars Pack, JVS Vehicles, and Real Racing Pack collectively add 200+ vehicles. The JVS Luxury Pack specifically targets BMW, Mercedes, Rolls-Royce, Bentley, and Aston Martin enthusiasts.

For players who want a complete car experience, pairing Immersive Vehicles with the Better Roads mod creates a proper driving environment. Add Dynmap for a real-time map of your server's road network. The combination turns Minecraft into a surprisingly convincing open-world driving simulator — all completely free and mod-supported.
